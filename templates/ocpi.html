OCPI Roaming ({{ partners|length }})

OCPI (Open Charge Point Interface) is the protocol that connects your charging network to other operators. When an eMSP (electric vehicle subscription provider) sends their drivers to your chargers, they authenticate via OCPI. This page manages those roaming partnerships — who you trust, what tokens they have, and whether the connection is live.
